Install

Install BibleSense

BibleSense opens from your browser, then installs onto your home screen to function as a regular app.

Takes less than a minute!

Progressive web app • No App Store Needed
Safari or Chrome

iPhone / iPad

Great in Safari or Chrome

  • Open BibleSense in Safari or Chrome
  • Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
  • Scroll and tap “Add to Home Screen”
  • Tap “Add”

If you don’t see it, try the other browser (Safari or Chrome).

Chrome or Safari

Android

Great in Chrome

  • Open BibleSense in Chrome
  • Tap the three-dot menu
  • Tap “Install app” or “Add to Home screen”
  • Confirm by tapping “Install” or “Add”

The wording may look slightly different depending on your phone.

Chrome, Safari, or Edge

Desktop / Laptop

Safari, Edge: you’re covered

  • Open BibleSense in Chrome or Safari (Edge works well too)
  • Look for the install icon in the address bar
  • Click “Install”
  • Open BibleSense from your desktop or app list

If no install icon appears, try Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

What’s a progressive web app?

A progressive web app is a website that can be installed like an app. It gives you a clean app-style experience without needing the App Store.

FAQ

Questions? We’ve got you.

Quick answers to help you get started with confidence.

What is BibleSense?

BibleSense is a Bible app that helps you understand what you’re reading as you go, with instant explanations, guided studies, and real-life scripture guidance.

Is BibleSense a replacement for church or a pastor?

No. BibleSense is a tool to support your personal reading and understanding. Your church community and spiritual leaders still matter.

How does the Scripture Guidance feature work?

You can type what you’re going through, and BibleSense points you to relevant Scripture and guidance rooted in the Bible. It’s designed to help you find direction, not replace wisdom or real conversations.

Do I need to download it from the App Store?

No. BibleSense works right from your browser. You can add it to your home screen in seconds, no app store needed.

How do I install it?

iPhone: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen

Android: Tap menu → Install App

Desktop: Click Install in your browser bar

Do I need an account?

Yes, an account lets you save your progress, journal entries, and personalized experience across devices.

What’s included in the free version?

The free plan includes core features with generous limits. Premium unlocks full access to studies, unlimited guidance, and more.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your data is private and never sold. Your reading, journal entries, and activity are stored securely.

Is this safe for younger users?

Yes. BibleSense is designed with younger users in mind and focuses on safe, supportive, and scripture-based guidance.
