Get the app on your device

Add BibleSense in a few taps

Same app—on your home screen. Nothing to download from an app store.

No App Store Needed

iPhone & iPad

  1. Open BibleSense in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow up).
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add—you’re ready to go.

Android

  1. Open BibleSense in Chrome.
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) in the corner.
  3. Tap Install or Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm—BibleSense sits with your other apps.

Computer

  1. Open BibleSense in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar when you see it.
  3. Or use the menu and choose Install BibleSense.
  4. Open it from your desktop or start menu—like any app.
