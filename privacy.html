Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

At BibleSense, your privacy matters, especially for younger users. We are committed to being transparent about what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable privacy laws.

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At BibleSense, your privacy matters. We designed this app to support Bible study, reflection, and spiritual growth while respecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name, if provided
  • Bible version preference
  • Reading progress and saved verses
  • Prayer journal entries
  • Study progress and quiz results
  • Subscription status

We do not collect your full name, phone number, home address, or government ID.

3. Children Under 13

BibleSense is designed to be safe for users as young as 10. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account without parental consent, contact support@zencora.org and we will help delete the account.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize your BibleSense experience
  • Save reading progress, saved verses, and prayer journal entries
  • Track study progress and quiz results
  • Process subscriptions securely
  • Send important account-related emails
  • Improve the app over time

We never sell your personal information. Ever.

5. Scripture Guidance Feature and Your Data

When you use the Real Talk: Scripture Guidance Feature, your input is securely processed by a trusted third-party service to generate a scripture-based response.

We do not permanently store the content of these interactions.

We recommend avoiding sharing highly sensitive personal information in any digital guidance tool.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using our backend services. We use encryption in transit and at rest where available, and privacy controls such as Row Level Security to help protect your information.

7. Third-Party Services

BibleSense uses trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase for database and authentication
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • OpenAI for scripture-based guidance processing
  • Resend for transactional emails

8. Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by emailing support@zencora.org.

We will process requests within 30 days when reasonably possible.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes.

10. Contact Us

Questions about your privacy?
